I'm a PhD student at the Université Libre de Bruxelles (ULB) under the supervision of Prof. Parente. During my PhD, I'm developing and improving machine learning models which optimize physical parameters for reacting flows.

During my engineering studies at the VUB, I discovered a passion for data science and machine learning, which I further explored through a second Master's in Statistics and Data Science at KU Leuven. Now, I focus on combining these two worlds by applying machine learning to engineering problems.
